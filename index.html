Nutritional Benefits of Water

When the TDS value is between 150 and 250 and the pH value is between 6.5 and 8.5, the water is generally considered optimal for drinking. This range indicates a good balance of essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium, which are beneficial for health.
